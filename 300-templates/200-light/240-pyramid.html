Technology Maturity Model

Optimise Continuous improvement
Measure Observability & feedback loops
Automate CI/CD, testing, and infrastructure as code
Standardise Consistent tooling, practices, and documentation across teams
Start at the bottom — you must standardise before you can automate. Ask the audience where they think their organisation currently sits on this pyramid. The goal isn't to reach "Optimise" quickly; it's to build each layer solidly before moving up.
