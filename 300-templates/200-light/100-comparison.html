Approach A vs Approach B

Option A — Migrate
  • Lower upfront cost, phased investment
  • Familiar tooling for existing team
  • Risk is isolated to migration windows
  • Reversible within first 6 months
Option B — Rebuild
  • Clean-slate architecture, no legacy debt
  • Enables new capabilities from day one
  • Better long-term performance ceiling
  • Stronger hiring story for engineers
Both options are viable — the decision hinges on risk appetite and timeline. If the room is leaning Option A, probe the long-term cost of carried tech debt. If leaning Option B, probe the resource requirement and disruption during rebuild.
