Finding 03

Execution gaps are eroding competitive advantage

Speed of delivery has become the primary differentiator in this market.

Supporting Evidence

01
Time-to-market has doubled since 2022 Increasing process overhead and approval chains account for 60 % of added delay.
02
Top performers ship 3× more frequently Benchmarking against industry peers shows a widening delivery cadence gap.
03
Engineering capacity is misallocated 47 % of dev hours are spent on rework, not net-new feature work.
