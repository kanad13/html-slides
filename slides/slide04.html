🔐 Your Privacy All slides stay on your machine. Works completely offline. Nothing is uploaded or shared.
Go deeper into the advanced features. Overview (press O) lets you jump around the deck in any order—great for handling questions. Speaker Notes (press N) keep your talking points private. Presenter Mode (press P) opens a separate window on a second monitor with your notes, a timer, and slide navigation. Theme toggle lets you adapt to the room's lighting. All of these work without any external tools or services.
