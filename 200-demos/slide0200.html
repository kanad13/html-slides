Three paths

Choose how you want to work.

HTML Deck Studio supports presenting, generating, and exporting—all in local HTML.

Present

Open 100-viewer.html, pick your slide folder, and present immediately. No installation, no dependencies.

Generate

Give an AI agent the templates folder. It creates standalone HTML slides with embedded tokens and CSS.

Export

Convert your slide folder to a full-bleed 16:9 PDF. Optional local Python toolchain, production ready.

Three paths are the core of HTML Deck Studio. Users can pick any combination based on their workflow.
