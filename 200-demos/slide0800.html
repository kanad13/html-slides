Quality gates

Generated decks need checks that match the contract.

1Validate structureCheck naming, doctype, titles, notes, inline CSS, and token names.
2Review visualsOpen the viewer, inspect light and dark chrome, and confirm overview and notes.
3Check theme disciplineKeep raw colors in token sections and use tokens in components.
4Record contextUpdate deck-context.md when narrative, theme, or source assumptions change.
